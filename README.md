# Supply Chain Analysis (Excel Visualization)

## Overview
This project analyzes the supply chain operations of a Fashion & Beauty company using Microsoft Excel. The dataset was cleaned, analyzed, and visualized to uncover insights related to revenue performance, inventory levels, shipping efficiency, transportation costs, and defect rates.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel

## Business Questions Answered
- How does product price relate to revenue?
- What percentage of total sales comes from each product type?
- Which shipping carriers generate the most revenue?
- What are the stock levels by SKU?
- How do transportation modes affect costs and defect rates?

## Key Insights
- A small number of SKUs contribute most of the revenue
- Transportation mode impacts both cost and defect rates
- Inventory levels vary significantly across products
